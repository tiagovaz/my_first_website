Our Story

Founded in 2018 on Rue Saint-Denis, Cafe Montreal started with one espresso machine and a lot of heart. We source beans from small farms, roast them in-house, and bake all our pastries from scratch before sunrise.

  • Single-origin beans roasted weekly
  • All pastries baked fresh before 6 AM
  • Free Wi-Fi and plenty of outlets
  • Dog-friendly patio in summer
